I plan on continuing to out work everybody and make it to the NBA in the future. This will not be easy but I am willing to work endlessly for this. Basketball is my world.

After July of 2018 I came into my senior year with 17 full-ride scholarship offers for basketball. Washington State was one of my top schools before I made my decision.
